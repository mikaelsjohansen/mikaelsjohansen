How can we minimize the impact of computer device production on the environment?
Companies can minimize the impact of computer device production on the environment by using energy efficient manufacturing processes and using sustainable materials. These changes will reduce carbon emissions being produced, and completely remove the need to destroy nature for mining natural resources.

What are some ways to reduce the environmental impact of computer use?
Conserving power can help reduce the amount of energy taken by computer use. Examples of this include turning off the device when not being used, and making sure to unplug the device when finished with your task. Another thing we can do is to extend the lifespan of the device. By making sure that the device lasts as long as possible, you are reducing the amount of e-waste being potentially polluting the environment.

How can we minimize the impact of computer disposal on the environment?
The biggest thing that you can do to help minimize the impact of computer disposal is to recycle old devices. Below is a list of ways you can help donate them.
  • Manufacturer take-back programs
  • Donate to charity
  • Local e-waste recycling programs
