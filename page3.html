Alarm

When the system detects that the door is opened (magnetic force is decreased), the system will activate an audio alarm resembling a police car siren. Any intruders attempting to do bad things to your home will likely be deterred by this alarm.
/

Light System

An alarm isn't the only awesome thing this system offers. A light switch system is also included, allowing you to turn on all the lights in your home the moment you enter the door. By pressing button A on the keypad, the light system will be activated, and button B will turn all the lights off.
