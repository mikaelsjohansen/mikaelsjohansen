Password and Door Lock Technology

This technology isn't the generic keypad password thing you commonly see. With this new product, you won't have to remember any complicated password combination. All you'll have to do is press two buttons at the same time. Once the password is entered correctly, the system will unlock the lock placed on your door. After 10 seconds, the system will automatically place the lock back in place, although you can always close the door and engage the lock manually.

Magnet Sensor Technology

The system has a built-in magnet sensor. When the door is closed, the magnetic force will be strong, indicating to the system that the door is closed. However, when the door is opened, the magnet sensor will detect a decrease in magnetic force, indicating to it that the door is opened.
